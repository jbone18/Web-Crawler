What is Tapped In Spokane?

Tapped In is a web app that provides a single point of contact to all tap house's and breweries in the greater Spokane area. This platform will provide users, who are 21 and over, the ability to enjoy the full experience of breweries in Spokane in a fashion never seen before. This seamless web app will be accessible on any device that has internet capabilities, making it available to anyone who has a smart phone, laptop, tablet or other device. This will allow both apple and android users to access the same quality content and decrease the amount of resources required to keep the platform up to date. The web app will be easily used by first time users as well as providing a consistently evolving and updated content for avid consumers. This exciting experience is accomplished by providing the following content.

A database of drinks that auto updates past, present and future with the option to create multiple user profiles. These user profiles will allow the use of a personalized drink rating system with push notifications of brewery events and new taps tailored to the user's interests. There will also be access to a map of brewery locations.

Features such as a personalized profile of drinks will encourage veteran users to continue using the web app because they will have an easily accessible list of their favorite and least favorite drinks. New users will be able to discovery new brewery's they haven't heard of and can use others reviews of drinks to determine what they want. Everyone who uses this web page will find a multitude of features that is applicable to there current situation.

A key and unique feature of this web app is its ability to auto-update both the drink list and brewery events. This auto-update ability will allow the user to encounter new content from one location rather than having to wait or double check a brewery's website to confirm events and drinks. This web app will also include the persona and culture of Spokane into its thematic layout. A customized and community theme/feature is what will truly set this web app apart from pre-existing sites which mainly give a sense of mass-social media appeal. In recent years, the desire for personalized, smaller community driven features has been driving the app market and is what Tapped In plans to take advantage of.
